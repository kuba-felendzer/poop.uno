Weatherman

A Discord bot that tells you the weather

This is a bot that I wrote so wouldn't have to keep sending screenshots of the weather in my personal servers. It uses the python-weather API by vierofernando, which is not the most accurate but it's very easy to use.

Along with telling you the weather, I also added an extra command that just spits out some text it picked from a list. This was just to quell my boredom while I was on a school trip, mostly.
